Introduction

Elara

Elara is a hair salon in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo offering services from balayage, perms, to hair treatments. Our hair stylists and colorists have extensive experience with Asian fine hair, specializing in Korean techniques. Elara's team is trained to focus on the client's goal while also ensuring that the hair is kept healthy and nourished.

Target audience

People who want to keep up with the hottest Korean hair trends will want to book with Elara. Teenagers and young adults are most likley going to visit the site. And since Elara is a luxury salon, customers are on middle to high income levels.

The visitor will come to the site to review the servies and stylists, submit a price quote, or book an appointment. They also might check where the salon is located or find the contact information.
